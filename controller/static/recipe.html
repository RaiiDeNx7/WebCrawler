Food Desert Recipes

Peanut Butter & Banana Toast

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Banana

Directions: Toast the bread until golden brown. Spread peanut butter evenly on the toast. Slice the banana and place it on top. Serve immediately.

Canned Bean Salad

  • Canned Beans
  • Olive Oil
  • Vinegar

Directions: Drain and rinse the canned beans. Mix them in a bowl with olive oil and vinegar. Stir well and serve chilled or at room temperature.

Instant Oatmeal with Fruit

  • Instant Oats
  • Dried or Canned Fruit
  • Honey

Directions: Prepare instant oats according to package instructions. Add dried or canned fruit on top. Drizzle with honey and mix well before eating.

Tuna & Crackers

  • Canned Tuna
  • Crackers
  • Mayonnaise (optional)

Directions: Open the canned tuna and mix with mayonnaise if desired. Spread the tuna on crackers and serve as a snack or light meal.

Microwave Scrambled Eggs

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Salt & Pepper

Directions: Beat eggs with milk, salt, and pepper in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until cooked through.

Ramen Noodle Stir-Fry

  • Instant Ramen
  • Canned Vegetables
  • Soy Sauce

Directions: Cook ramen noodles without the seasoning packet. Drain and stir-fry with canned vegetables and a splash of soy sauce.

Mac and Cheese with Tuna

  • Boxed Mac and Cheese
  • Canned Tuna
  • Milk

Directions: Prepare mac and cheese according to package instructions. Stir in drained canned tuna before serving.

Rice and Beans

  • Instant Rice
  • Canned Beans
  • Salsa

Directions: Cook instant rice and mix with drained canned beans and salsa for a simple, hearty meal.

Vegetable Soup

  • Canned Vegetables
  • Vegetable Broth
  • Seasoning

Directions: Heat canned vegetables and broth in a pot. Add seasoning to taste and serve warm.

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Directions: Butter the bread and place cheese between slices. Cook in a pan over medium heat until golden brown on both sides.

Pancakes with Syrup

  • Pancake Mix
  • Water
  • Syrup

Directions: Prepare pancakes according to mix instructions. Serve with syrup.

Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Directions: Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread and jelly on the other. Press together and serve.
